Frequently Asked Questions

 Will pay the price i was quoted?

Yes, the price quoted is the price you’ll pay for service you requested in most of the cases. In some cases, your final price may go down or up after mechanic perform the initial diagnosis. Don’t worry, if there’s any additional work required, the mechanic will contact you and get your consent prior to starting. 

 What type of mechanics I can request on MechHub?

You have two options, you can either request mobile mechanic or visit repair shop. 

 What are my payment options?

Pay the repair shop or mobile mechanic once your car service and/or repairs have been completed. Please check which payment options are available on your mechanic's detail page or ask your mechanic using comment feature on your booking detail page. 

 Can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel an appointment online, call the mechanic directly or contact the MechHub representative. 

 Can I change my appointment date & time?

You can change your appointment by contacting your mechanic using comment feature inside the booking detail page, calling the mechanic directly or contacting the MechHub representative.
