How MechHub Works?

Follow four simple steps

Customer submits a request

Provide car and location information, preferred appointment time and service required

Submit a reasonable quote

Submit a quote manually or set up the auotmated bidding system to send the quote automatically

Booking confirmed

Receive booking confirmation when the customer accepts your quote and confirm the booking with you

Perform service and get paid

On the day of the appointment, perform high-quality work, recieve payment from the customer and confirm service completion on MechHub
